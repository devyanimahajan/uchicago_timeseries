D: 10 pts What is the advantage of this VAR model? What would ARIMA or prophet models on this dataset look like, if we were asked to forecast AHE?

  • VAR is the best choice when forecasting AHE in relation to other indicators like PCE and PCEPI, as it captures interdependencies.
  • ARIMA works well for purely autoregressive forecasting, but it ignores external variables. While ARIMA can work well when AHE follows an autoregressive pattern, it does not incorporate the effects of PCE and PCEPI, which could reduce its accuracy in capturing macroeconomic trends.
  • Prophet is great for long-term trends and seasonal forecasting, but may not be the most suitable for analysis where relationships between variables matter.
